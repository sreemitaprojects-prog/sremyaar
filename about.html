About

I am nineteen, a full time college student studying political science, and I am figuring it out in public.

I write and I build, which is a strange combination until it isn't. By day I work at Decoding Draupadi, a media and speaker platform for urban Indian working women, where I do a bit of everything — content, editorial, product, strategy. Outside of that I make things: maps, tools, websites, the occasional book. I do all of this while attending lectures on constitutional law and international relations, which I think makes me better at both.

I care about Indian women, Indian media, and the infrastructure that makes stories findable and speakable. I think the most interesting work happens at the intersection of those three things, and that is where I am trying to plant myself.

I am based in Kolkata, usually online, always thinking about something.
